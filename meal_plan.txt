Weekly Meal Plan for Carolyn, Sebastian, and You\n\n**Day 1**\n- Breakfast: Oatmeal topped with fresh berries and a drizzle of honey\n- Snack: Sliced apple with almond butter\n- Lunch: Quinoa salad with cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and feta cheese\n- Snack: Carrot sticks with hummus\n- Dinner: Grilled chicken breast with steamed broccoli and sweet potatoes\n\n**Day 2**\n- Breakfast: Smoothie with asparagus and brown rice\n\n**Day 3**\n- Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with spinach and whole-grain toast\n- Snack: Orange slices\n- Lunch: Chickpea salad with bell peppers and avocado\n- Snack: Mixed nuts\n- Dinner: Stir-fried tofu with mixed vegetables and quinoa\n\n**Day 4**\n- Breakfast: Chia seed pudding with coconut milk and mango\n- Snack: Cucumber slices with tzatziki\n- Lunch: Turkey and avocado wrap in a whole-grain tortilla\n- Snack: Hard-boiled eggs\n- Dinner: Beef stir-fry with broccoli and brown rice\n\n**Day 5**\n- Breakfast: Smoothie bowl with banana, spinach, and granola\n- Snack: Berries\n- Lunch: Grilled vegetable sandwich on whole-grain bread\n- Snack: Popcorn (air-popped)\n- Dinner: Baked chicken thighs with roasted Brussels sprouts and quinoa\n\n**Day 6**\n- Breakfast: Whole-grain pancakes topped with fresh fruit\n- Snack: Trail mix\n- Lunch: Spinach salad with grilled shrimp and citrus dressing\n- Snack: Bell pepper strips with guacamole\n- Dinner: Zucchini noodles with marinara sauce and turkey meatballs\n\n**Day 7**\n- Breakfast: Yogurt parfait with granola and mixed berries\n- Snack: Sliced pear\n- Lunch: Quinoa and black bean bowl with
